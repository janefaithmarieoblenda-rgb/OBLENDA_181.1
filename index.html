About Me

Hello! My name is Jane! My online alias is jenky. I'm an artist from the Philippines. I work for content creators as a side gig, and I'm also one of the content creators in the official MLBB page.

I grew up in Paulina Homes, Dalipuga, Iligan City with two older brothers. Their influence introduced me to the internet, games, and software tinkering. Over time, I discovered my passion for art and began pursuing it as a hobby during elementary school.

My goal in life is to become a well-known artist, secure a stable job, and reconnect with my childhood friends who live far away from Mindanao.

Hobbies & Interests

I have always been passionate about art since I was young and never expected to pursue a career with it. I also love playing games whenever I get the chance, especially when I'm with friends—it makes the experience even more enjoyable.
